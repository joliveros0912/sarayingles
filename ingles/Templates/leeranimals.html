Reading Activity - Animals

Animals are part of our everyday lives. Some live in our homes, like dogs and cats, while others live in the wild, like lions and elephants. Dogs are loyal and love to be around people. They bark, run, and play. Cats are quiet and like to sleep in warm places. They also enjoy being near their owners. Birds are small animals that can fly and sing. Many people keep birds as pets because of their beautiful colors and songs.
