The Seasons of the Year

Our planet Earth has four very special seasons: spring, summer, autumn, and winter. Each season is different and brings us wonderful things.

In spring, everything is filled with color. Flowers begin to bloom, birds sing, and the weather becomes warmer. It's as if nature is awakening from a long sleep!

Then comes summer, the hottest season of the year. The sun shines brightly, the days are long, and the nights are short. It's perfect for going to the beach and eating ice cream.

Then, the leaves on the trees change color and fall in autumn. The orange, red, and yellow hues create a beautiful landscape. The weather becomes cooler.

Finally, winter arrives, the coldest season. Sometimes it snows, and we bundle up with scarves and hats. It's time to drink hot drinks and play in the snow.
