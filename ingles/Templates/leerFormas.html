The Shapes Play in the Park

Circle rolled happily. Square walked firmly. Triangle hopped happily. Rectangle slid smoothly. They were friends and loved to play in the Shapes Park.

In the park, they saw Oval and Star. They played hide-and-seek behind the round trees and built a tall tower with the square blocks. Triangle climbed a pointy slide. Rectangle found a long swing.

Even though they were different, they had a lot of fun together. Circle rolled fast, Square made strong bases, Triangle pointed high, and Rectangle was long. Being different made them a great team!
