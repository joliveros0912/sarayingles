The Seasons of the Year

Spring: It's when the flowers begin to bloom, the birds sing, and everything is filled with color. It's as if nature has awakened from a long sleep! Summer: It's very warm, the sun shines brightly, and it's the perfect time to play outdoors, go to the beach, and eat ice cream. Autumn: The leaves on the trees change color and fall, creating carpets of orange, red, and yellow hues. The weather gets cooler, and we prepare for winter. Winter: It's very cold, sometimes it snows, and we bundle up with scarves and hats. It's a time to enjoy hot drinks, play in the snow, and spend time indoors.
